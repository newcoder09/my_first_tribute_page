DR Anne Marie Imafidon MBE

Inspiring young girls to take up TECH

All about DR Anne Marie:

  • At age 11, she passed her first A Level.
  • She read Maths and Computer Science at Oxford, she was just 20 years old when she graduated with a Masters Degree.
  • She worked at many Blue Chip companies including Goldman Sachs and Hewlett Packard.
  • She has honorary doctorates from The Open University and The University of Kent
  • She has an Honorary Fellowship from Keble College, Oxford.
  • All this experience allowed her to set up a social initiative Stemettes.
  • More than 50 000 girls have been exposed to tech due Stemettes. Her goal is to inspire and promote the next generation of young women in STEM.
  • She was awarded an MBE in 2017, as a respected thought-leader in the tech space.

DR Anne Marie is well known on the speakers circuit. She has a wealth of expertise, her main topics are Tech, Social and youth Entrepreneurship, also diversity. You can also regularly hear her on popular podcasts such as Evening Standard and has also appeared on Countdown and numerous tv programmes.

"Introducing children to the work of female STEM role models is an important way to inspire girls and young woment into STEM careers while challenging stiffling industry stereotypes"
- DR Anne Marie Imafidon MBE
